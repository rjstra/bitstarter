Its that time of the month again. Expenses time.

You've entertained all those clients and driven all those miles, now its time to claim the money all back. But wait. Where are the receipts for the lunch with those guys from that internet startup. Gone and the money with them.

Or is it? With PocketBook you can record your expenses as you go and accounting will automatically see the totals at the end of the month with no work from you. Never loose those receipts again.

PocketBook is a mobile app that busy people can use to record their business expenses and have them automatically submitted to their accounts department.
  • Record receipts and mielage
  • Classify your expense items by role/customer account
  • Record time spent with clients for billing purposes
